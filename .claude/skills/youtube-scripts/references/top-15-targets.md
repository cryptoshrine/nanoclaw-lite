# Top 15 Outreach Targets for Loom Campaign

## Selection Criteria

Each target was selected based on:
- **Content Focus:** Tactical Analysis (primary requirement)
- **Audience Size:** 50K-500K (large enough to matter, small enough to respond)
- **Engagement Rate:** >5% average
- **Platform:** Twitter/X active (fastest communication)
- **Recent Activity:** Posted in last 7 days
- **StatsBomb Relevance:** Already uses data in content

## Response Handling

### Positive Response → VIP Track
- Skip standard nurture sequence
- Direct personal follow-up within 2 hours
- Route to Partnership Selection page
- Schedule onboarding call within 48 hours

### Neutral Response → Nurture Track
- Add to email sequence
- Continue Twitter engagement
- Follow up with value content

### No Response → Touch 2/3
- Day 3: Personalized asset follow-up
- Day 7: Final direct ask
- After Day 7: Move to long-term nurture

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Loom videos sent | 15-20 |
| Response rate | 25%+ (4+ responses) |
| Response-to-signup | 50%+ (2+ signups per week) |
| Founding Member conversions | 5-10 from outreach alone |

## Backup Targets (If Primary Targets Non-Responsive)

If response rate is below 20% by Week 2:
- Expand to FPL-focused creators (secondary ICP)
- Target smaller tactical accounts (5K-20K followers)
- Increase Twitter organic engagement
- Consider cold DM outreach to warm leads

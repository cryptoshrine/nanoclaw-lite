# Ball-AI Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

## Primary ICP: Tactical Analysis Content Creators

### Demographics

| Attribute | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Age | 25-40 years old |
| Gender | Predominantly male (85%+), growing female segment |
| Location | UK (primary), Europe, USA |
| Role | Football analyst, tactical blogger, analytics YouTuber |
| Platforms | Twitter/X (primary), YouTube (secondary), Instagram, TikTok |
| Audience | 10K-500K followers |
| Income | Part-time creator income (USD 0-20,000/year from content) |

### Psychographics

**Goals:**
- Build credibility through data-backed analysis
- Grow audience and engagement
- Monetize content (Patreon, YouTube, sponsorships)
- Stand out from opinion-based competitors
- Save time on analysis to publish more frequently

**Core Anxiety: Credibility Debt**
They know their football knowledge is solid, but competitors with Wyscout/StatsBomb access have authority they lack. Status-conscious about being seen as "serious" analysts vs casual fans.

**Motivations:**
- Passion for football tactics and analysis
- Desire for recognition in football community
- Building towards full-time creator career
- Proving insights through data, not just opinions

**Fears:**
- Being proven wrong due to lack of data
- Competitors publishing first
- Investing time in analysis that doesn't resonate
- Looking unprofessional compared to outlets

### Pain Points (Use in Scripts)

| Pain Point | Emotional Trigger | Ball-AI Solution |
|------------|-------------------|------------------|
| Can't access enterprise data | Credibility anxiety | StatsBomb data access |
| Wyscout costs £40K+/year | Financial exclusion | £59-99/month pricing |
| Manual analysis takes 4-6 hours | Opportunity cost | 5-minute generation |
| ChatGPT hallucinates stats | Professional embarrassment | Zero-hallucination, event-traced |
| Competitors publish faster | Fear of irrelevance | Instant post-match analysis |

### Current Solutions They Use

- FBref for raw statistics
- Understat for xG data
- Manual chart creation in Excel/Canva
- Watching matches multiple times for patterns

### Tools/Platforms Used

- Twitter/X for distribution
- YouTube for long-form content
- Canva for basic graphics
- Excel/Google Sheets for data
- OBS for video recording

### Decision-Making Process

- Price-sensitive but willing to pay for time savings
- Wants free trial before committing
- Influenced by other creators' recommendations
- Values social proof (testimonials, case studies)

### Budget Authority

- Self-funded or small creator budget
- Monthly subscription preference over annual
- Sweet spot: USD 15-50/month (£59-79/month for Ball-AI)

### Buying Journey

- Discovery to trial: 3-7 days
- Trial to paid: 14-30 days

### Communication Preferences

**Channels:**
- Twitter/X (primary discovery)
- YouTube (tutorials, reviews)
- Discord communities
- Football analytics Slack/Discord servers

**Content Formats:**
- Short Twitter threads showing results
- YouTube tutorials demonstrating features
- Before/after comparisons
- Case study blog posts

### Communities

- Football Twitter/X community
- Friends of Tracking (YouTube)

### Influencers They Follow

- Statman Dave

### Trigger Events (When They Search for Solutions)

- Competitor publishes better analysis
- Major match with desire for quick turnaround
- Criticism of opinion-based takes
- New season starting with motivation to improve
- Reaching follower milestone (1K, 5K, 10K)
- First monetization opportunity

### Seasonal Patterns

- Peak demand: August-September (season start), January (transfer window)
- Steady demand during season
- Lower demand: June-July (off-season)

| Attribute | Primary (Content Creator) | Secondary (Tactical Analyst) |
|-----------|--------------------------|------------------------------|
| Content Type | Short-form, social-first | Long-form (2,000+ word blogs, 15-30 min videos) |
| Knowledge Depth | Strong tactical awareness | Deep tactical knowledge (formations, pressing triggers) |

| Buying Cycle | 3-7 days to trial | Thorough evaluation |

### Demographics

- Age: 28-55 years old
- Platforms: Blog (primary), YouTube, Podcast, Twitter/X
- Audience: 1,000-25,000 regular readers/viewers
- Background: Former players, coaches, or football enthusiasts

### Value Proposition

- Deep tactical analysis with 360 freeze frame data
- Professional visualizations for blog/video embeds
- Match reports as foundation for editorial content
- Competitive advantage over peers using manual methods
